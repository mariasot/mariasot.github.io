Taylor Swift

an american singer

Bio

Taylor Swift was born on December 13,1989 in Pennsylvania. She began songwriting professionally at age 14 and signed with Big Machine Records in 2005 to become a country musician. Under Big Machine, she released six studio albums, four of them to country radio, starting with her self-titled album (2006).In 2018 she signed with Republic Records. Under Republic Records she released 4 studio albums and 3 album re-recordings.Having sold over 200 million records globally, Swift is one of the best-selling musicians, the most streamed woman on Spotify, and the only act to have five albums open with over one million copies sold in the US. She has appeared in 61 music videos, 13 of which she self-directed, and released five documentaries, three video albums, and a 10 minute short film, as well as been featured in numerous films and television shows. Since the beginning of her career, Taylor Swift has been nominated for over 500 awards, and has won 324.
